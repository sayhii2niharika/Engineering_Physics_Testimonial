CHITTI AVINASH REDDY
Engineering Physics
BTech 2020

Best memories in campus?
I have had some of the best memories of my life on this campus, from vibing with friends in Ghat to taking trips across the north east. But, I would say the best feeling is when I just drown my self in the lovely roast battles which take place in hostels....Nothing beats hanging out with the boys...
Biggest change since first year?
Engineering life has taught me very little about engineering but a lot about life. Each semester morphed my mindset and perspective, I truly was 8 different people, one in each semester here at IITG. Mainly, I grew more confident and learnt to stay unfazed in the middle of troublesome situations.
Best thing you learned from here?
Best thing I learnt on campus was that people are not so different from you and me. As I met a diverse range of people, I found comfort in the fact that we as humans are all learning about life everyday...
One thing you'll miss the most?
I will miss the comfort of having my people around me. IIT Guwahati truly was a home away from home for me and I will genuinely miss everyone once I go to an unknown city with unknown people around....
One thing you regret not doing?
I regret not taking risks in life....There were many points throughout my engineering life where I had the opportunity to step up and take up responsibility but I failed to do so....From Class BR to Senator to Club Secy to Gen Sec, a little regret resides inside me that I didn't take that shot!
